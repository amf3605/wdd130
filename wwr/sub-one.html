Is this a good trip for kids? What are the allowed age ranges?

Absolutely! This is a good trip for children of all ages. We have found that children over the age of 8 enjoy it most. A common concern we get from parents is that children will be bored stuck on a boat for hours at a time, but our guides make it fun for all ages.

Is white water rafting safe? I don't even know how to swim!

Yes! Safety is our top priority. Our guides are highly trained and each guest receives a life jacket and safety instruction before boarding. Many non-swimmers raft with us every year.
